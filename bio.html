Hey, My Name Is Chelsea!

I am a Graphic and Web Designer, fresh out on the market for employment. I stumbled upon my interest in Web Design through the Graphic Design BFA program at the Art Institute, and now I'm hooked! I have a natural relationship with the visual world, and I greatly enjoy learning more about Web Development.

My relationship with art began at a young age, and has been encouraged by friends and family throughout my whole life. I love telling stories with doodles, so it seemed obvious that I would go to school to find a way to better market my talents. I never expected to love something as intimidating as code and Web Development. Once it was broken down for me, I found the challenge encouraging. It exercises a part of my brain, and it allows me to problem solve. I love puzzles!

Along with puzzles, I enjoy video games, board games, comic book, cult movies, and cartoons! Thankfully, my son seems to enjoy these things too. Ultimately my goal is to find work doing something I enjoy, that challenges me and pushes me to learn more. I want to pursue a career that gives me time to be with my kid, and provide him with a stable home. Pretty reasonable, don't you think?
